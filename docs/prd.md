Product Requirements Document (PRD)
HomeHelp Connect
Version: 1.0
Status: MVP
________________________________________
1. Product Overview
Product Name
HomeHelp Connect
Tagline
Find Help. Find Work.
Vision
HomeHelp Connect aims to connect residents with trusted household workers through a simple, mobile-friendly platform.
The platform initially focuses on helping residents find workers such as maids, cooks, baby-care helpers, and elder-care helpers.
The platform is designed to scale from communities and apartment complexes to cities and regions.
________________________________________
2. Problem Statement
Residents often struggle to find reliable household workers.
Current methods include:
•	WhatsApp groups
•	Word-of-mouth recommendations
•	Community notice boards
•	Personal references
These methods are fragmented and inefficient.
Workers also struggle to discover new work opportunities and rely heavily on personal referrals.
There is no simple platform that connects both sides efficiently.

5. MVP Scope
The MVP focuses on connecting residents and workers.
Included
•	Resident Registration
•	Worker Registration
•	Worker Profiles
•	Worker Search
•	Availability Management
•	Requirement Posting
•	Ratings and Reviews
•	Interest Requests
•	Saved Workers
•	WhatsApp and Call Integration
Excluded
•	Online Payments
•	Chat System
•	Agencies
•	Push Notifications
•	Aadhaar Verification
•	Police Verification
•	Premium Memberships
•	Subscription Plans
________________________________________

Resident
Can:
•	Register/Login

•	View Profiles
•	Call Workers

•	Post Requirements
•	Submit Reviews

•	Disable Accounts
________________________________________
7. Core Features
Worker Discovery
Residents can search workers using:
•	Service Type
•	Time Slot
•	Looking For Work Status
________________________________________
Worker Profiles
Profiles contain:
•	Photo
•	Name
•	Services
•	Charges
•	Availability
•	Experience
•	Reviews
•	Trust Indicators
________________________________________
Requirement Marketplace
Residents can post requirements.
Workers can browse requirements and contact residents.
Requirements automatically expire after 30 days.
________________________________________
Interest Requests
Residents can mark a worker as “Interested”.
Workers can view interested residents from their dashboard.
________________________________________
Ratings & Reviews
Residents can:
•	Rate workers
•	Leave reviews
Workers build reputation over time.
________________________________________
8. Trust & Safety
The platform should display:
•	Mobile Verified Badge
•	Experience Information
•	References (Optional)
•	Ratings
•	Reviews
New workers should display:
“New Profile”
instead of a low rating.
________________________________________
9. Geographic Model
The platform supports:
Country → State → City → Area → Community (Optional)
This allows future scaling without changing architecture.
________________________________________
10. Languages
Supported Languages:
•	English
•	Tamil
The platform should be bilingual and mobile-friendly.
________________________________________
11. Communication
Communication occurs outside the platform.
Methods:
•	Phone Call
•	WhatsApp
No internal chat is included in the MVP.
________________________________________
12. Business Model
MVP Phase:
Free
Future Monetization Options:
•	Featured Profiles
•	Premium Listings
•	Subscriptions
•	Sponsored Profiles
Monetization decisions will be made after user adoption.
________________________________________
13. Success Metrics
The MVP is successful if:
•	Workers can create profiles.
•	Residents can discover workers.
•	Residents can contact workers.
•	Residents can post requirements.
•	Workers can discover opportunities.
•	Reviews are being collected.
•	Successful worker-resident connections occur.
________________________________________
14. Risks
User Adoption
Workers may be hesitant to register.
________________________________________
Trust
Residents may prefer referrals over new workers.
________________________________________
Data Quality
Incomplete profiles may reduce platform usefulness.
________________________________________
Scalability
Future city-wide expansion may require additional moderation and verification systems.
________________________________________
15. MVP Release Goal
Launch a simple platform where:
•	Workers can advertise services.
•	Residents can discover workers.
•	Residents and workers can connect directly.
No advanced marketplace features are required for the first release.
The primary objective is validating demand and facilitating successful worker-resident connections.
